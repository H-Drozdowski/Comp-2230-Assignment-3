# Adventure Plan

## Setting

The setting of the game will be a mid evil fantasy dungeon.

## Entrance

It will start you outside of the dungeon with only the option to enter or turn away.

## The Dungeon

A main hall with three doors two on the left and right side on in front.

The door in front -> a long hallway with a dead end.
The left door -> A dark room filled with giant spiders. 
The right door -> A huge room full of statues.

### Long hallway

choice 1 -> pull the lever. -> fall into a pit trap bellow you. 
choice 2 -> feel around in the dark for a secret button. -> opens a secret door at the end of the hallway with treasure in it. 

### Spider room

choice 1 -> fight the spiders -> loose.
choice 2 -> quickly close the door -> safely return to the main room.

### Statue room

choice 1 -> grab the big jewel on the podium in the middle of the room. -> statues come to life and attack. 
choice 2 -> leave the room. -> safely return to the main room.
